Welcome to PiplMesh's documentation!
====================================
PiplMash is one of the projects of students at Faculty of computer and information science (http://www.fri.uni-lj.si)
and wlan slovenija (http://wlan-si.net) open wireless network.
Its main goal is the development of an open source application for wlan slovenija's welcome page.
